About Me

I created my first webpage in the mid-90s and shortly thereafter became the de facto webmaster for the administrative department where I worked at Northwestern University. I built that original site using just html and javascript but a few years later I was building and managing sites using CSS, MySQL and PHP. Then a career move suddenly ended my days as webmaster, but opened the door to other coding opportunities. Now I'm hoping to brush off those cobwebs, and merge my past experience with my current expertise to become a full stack web developer.
